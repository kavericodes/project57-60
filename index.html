Dance!

My hobby is dancing! I love dancing because it gives me joy, when I'm bored I just dance! I have been dancing ever since I was 3 years old. My favorite styles of dance are acro and ballet. Acro is short for Acrobatics. Acrobatics is doing all different types of tricks and flips! I love doing handstands, cartwheels, roundoffs, and one-handed cartwheels. My second favorite style of dance is ballet! Ballet is the most traditional style of dance in my opinion. Ballet can show of your technique, turns, and jumps! I take ballet classes 4:30-6:00 on Tuesdays and I practice acro tricks all the time! I started doing the Nutcracker when I was 5 or 6 years old. We perform in December and rehearse September - December. I have lots of friends in dance who are very fun and kind. When we have Nutcracker rehearsals we always stick together! That is all about my favorite hobby!
